Front End Development Challenge

I've created this space to practice and strengthen my HTML and CSS skills. My dream is to one day become a UX designer, but for now I want to have fun and explore what I can create.

Part of what I love so much about web development is the mixture of logic and creativity involved. I love finding fonts, color palettes, and web layouts that speak perfectly to a brand's core values.

As a way to challenge myself, I asked ChatGPT to give me a healthy 100 prompts for web dev projects. It's a mild goal, but as much as I'd like to achieve a brand page in a day, I'd also like for it to match my creative vision so I'll give myself up to three days to plan, design, and perfect the project. I'll strikethrough each project I complete. See you on the other side :)
